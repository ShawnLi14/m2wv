Hi Remote workers! If you have the flexibility of working from home, you may want to relocate to a place with where tax rates and living expenses are low, and life quality is high. Look no further - West Virgina has everything you dream of!

If you are unfamilar with West Virginia and need help research where to live, use this tool! It is designed to make the best choice for you. Our algorithm considers a lot of factors, from school ratings to tax rates. We reference various data sources, such as US Census data, greatshcools.com, and city-data.com.

This web application is built with Python Flask framework and uses technologies like Census API, Bootstrap, AJAX and MySQL.
